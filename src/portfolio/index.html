Room-Ease was a centralized housing portal that I prototyped with a group of 5. This project aimed to better improve the finding a roommate experience, as well as provide a place to post subleases and other housing availibilites that are student friendly. In this project I mainly worked on the frontend of the roommates page, and helped fix the frontend of the home page, login, and signup page.
